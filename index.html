Understanding the Demand Curve

This curve visualizes consumer behavior according to the linear function inverse:

P = 20 - 2Qd

Notice how changing values visually changes the total area under the curve—this represented region equals total consumer expenditure or business revenue.

Analysis Tasks:

  • Find the price point where Total Revenue reaches its absolute maximum size ($Q=5$).
  • Observe the Choke Price ($P=20$): At this price threshold, quantity drops exactly to zero.
